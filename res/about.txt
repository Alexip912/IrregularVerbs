Hi, you are welcomed by an irregular verb simulator.

The infinitive form of the verb will be displayed to you, and you will have to enter the two remaining forms of the verb
after which you will see the words in which you made a mistake or a message stating that everything is correct.

After entering all forms of verbs, you get the result of your attempt as a percentage.

Enter all forms of the verb strictly from the lowercase letters of the English alphabet.

Course project called "IrregularVerbs", authors Petrov Daniil, Vladislav Fadin and Alexey Shkuratov.
